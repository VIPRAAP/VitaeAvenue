Common Questions
Can I change my stream after 11th? +
Yes, but it's challenging and involves restarting academic preparation. This is why choosing the right stream from the beginning is critical. Our counselling prevents regretful switches. Some lateral pathways exist — for example, a Science student can move to Commerce streams for MBA — but early planning is always better.
My child got 80%+ in 10th — should they take Science? +
Good marks don't mean Science is the right fit. Many high-scoring students thrive better in Commerce or Arts streams based on their actual aptitudes and interests. Marks measure memory and effort, not intelligence type or career fit. Our counselling reveals the deeper picture.
Is Arts stream a "lesser" choice? +
Absolutely not. India's top judges, journalists, designers, psychologists, civil servants, and entrepreneurs have Arts backgrounds. The perception that Arts is inferior is completely outdated. Some of the highest-paying careers today — UX design, content strategy, law, psychology — are rooted in Arts education.
